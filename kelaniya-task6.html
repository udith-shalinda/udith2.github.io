Diploma in Pāli and Buddhist Studies for foreign students commences– (27th Batch)

The Inauguration Ceremony of the Diploma in Pāli and Buddhist Studies (27th Intake) conducted by the Department of Pali and Buddhist Studies, Faculty of Humanities, University of Kelaniya was successfully held on the 01st of February 2018 at the Board Room of the Faculty of Humanities. The event was held under the patronage of Professor Patrick Ratnayake, Dean of the Faculty of Humanities, while Dr. Prashanthi Narangoda, Head of the Department of Fine Arts participated as the Chief Guest. In addition, the event was graced by Venerable Professor Therele Dhammarathana Thera, Head of the Department of Pali and Buddhist Studies, academic staff of the Department of Pali and Buddhist Studies and new foreign participants of the Diploma.
