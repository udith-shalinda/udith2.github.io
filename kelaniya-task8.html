Appreciation of Service at the Retirement of Dr. Indrika Abeygunawardena

A felicitation ceremony organised by the Department of Microbiology of the Faculty of Science, University of Kelaniya to appreciate the service of Dr. Indrika Abeygunawardena at her retirement was held on the 19th of February 2018 at the Board Room of the Faculty of Science. The ceremony was held with the objective of appreciating the immense service rendered by Dr. Indrika Abeygunawardena to the University and the society. The felicitation ceremony was held under the patronage of Professor D.M. Semasinghe, Vice-Chancellor of the University of Kelaniya. The event was graced by Professor Patrick Ratnayake, Dean of the Faculty of Humanities, Mr. Mahendra Gunawardena, Head of the Department of Microbiology, academic and non-academic staff members of the Faculty of Science and many undergraduates.
